About Grameen Bazaar

Grameen Bazaar is a platform dedicated to showcasing and preserving India's rich cultural heritage through handcrafted products. We connect rural artisans directly with customers worldwide, eliminating middlemen and ensuring fair compensation for their skills and craftsmanship.

For Artisans

Join our growing community of rural craftspeople and gain access to a global market. Our platform offers easy product listing, order management, and analytics to help sustain traditional crafts and improve livelihoods.

For Customers

Discover authentic Indian handicrafts made with generations of skill and tradition. Each purchase supports rural communities and helps preserve cultural heritage while bringing unique, handcrafted items to your home.
